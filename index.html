Our Story

Crafted Over Fire.
Served With Energy.

Born from a passion for authentic Texas BBQ, Steam Bar brings the art of slow-smoked meats to the heart of Tashkent. Our brisket spends 12+ hours over post oak, developing a bark that whispers tradition and tenderness that speaks for itself.

But we're more than BBQ. As the sun sets, Steam transforms — DJ sets pulse through our outdoor terrace, craft cocktails flow from our bar, and Tashkent's nightlife finds its home.
